NoPayStation
download vita games straight from the playstation server. a bit more complicated to set up than than just downloading from a rom site but safer and has a built in unpackager. follow the ‘configuration and usage’ section, following the areas that say ‘PSV’. you can also download .pkg files straight from their site under the browse section, which also has zRIF codes/work.bin files (vita3k will ask you for one of these when you install a game through the .pkg method).
